Hi, this website will tell you about all the basics of Thunkable

First, create an acount on Thunkable. You can do this by clicking on the image below and singning in through any of the options given. It is reccomended to sign in with google.

Then, click on the create new app button and name your project.

Then, it will take you to the project. You will be on the design page. On the left shall be all the components of the design and on the right shall be their properties. You can add the components by dragging and dropping them to the screen which will be in the middle. Then, you can change the properties on the right.

Then, you can see a section called blocks on the top of the page. That is where all the coding will be done. So, click on it and it will take you to the blocks section. You will fing stuff like, "Event" or "Logic" written on the left side. You have to click on those and you will find the coding blocks. You can drag and drop those to the screen.

And those are all the simple basics of thunkable.
